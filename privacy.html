Privacy Policy for Antigravity IDE Cockpit

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle user data and information.

1. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data or private information from users. The application runs entirely on your local machine.

2. Google OAuth & Scopes

  • The Google OAuth cloud-platform scope is strictly used on the user's local machine to call official Google APIs to retrieve developer quota information.
  • All access tokens and user profile information (email, profile) are stored locally in the user's secure IDE environment.
  • No user tokens, credentials, or private data are ever transmitted to any third-party external servers.

3. Security

Since all operations and data storage are local to your IDE client, the security of your tokens depends on your local system's security.

4. Contact Us

For any questions or issues regarding this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
